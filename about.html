About Me

Get to know more about Narasimha Reddy

Hello! I am Narasimha Reddy, an experienced software developer specializing in backend development. With a strong foundation in Java and extensive knowledge of database solutions, I am proficient in designing and implementing scalable and robust web applications. My journey in software development began with a passion for solving complex problems and creating efficient solutions.

I hold a master's degree in computer science with a concentration in software development from DePaul University. In addition to my technical expertise, I am committed to continuous learning and improvement. I actively participate in open source projects and enjoy sharing my knowledge through blogs and community engagements. When I am not coding, I like to stay active with fitness routines and spend time exploring new places.

Feel free to explore my projects and get in touch if you'd like to collaborate or learn more about my work.
